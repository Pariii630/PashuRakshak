# 🐄 PashuRakshak_AI – Early Disease Detection for Cattle

## 🚀 Overview

PashuRakshak_AI is an AI-powered mobile-first solution designed to help farmers detect diseases in cows and buffaloes at an early stage using just an image.

In rural areas, delayed diagnosis of diseases like Lumpy Skin Disease (LSD), Foot and Mouth Disease (FMD), and Mastitis leads to severe financial losses and animal mortality. Our solution empowers farmers with instant, accessible, and affordable disease insights.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

* Lack of access to timely veterinary care in rural areas
* Manual disease detection leads to delays and errors
* High economic losses due to late diagnosis
* Limited digital tools for livestock health monitoring

---

## 💡 Our Solution

PashuRakshak_AI uses **computer vision and AI** to:

* 📸 Analyze images of livestock
* 🧠 Detect early signs of disease
* ⚡ Provide instant predictions
* 📋 Suggest actionable next steps

---

## 🧠 Key Features

* ✅ AI-based disease detection from images
* ✅ Works in low-connectivity environments (edge AI ready)
* ✅ Simple and user-friendly interface
* ✅ Instant diagnosis + recommendations
* ✅ Scalable for multiple diseases
